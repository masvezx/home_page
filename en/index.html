Basic information

Hi! My name is Maria (friends and family call me Masha), and I’d like to tell you a little about myself. I was born in Moscow, lived there for about eleven years, then spent two years in Serbia, and now I live in Germany. Each move gave me new experiences, friends, and memories.

After moving to Serbia, I started playing the guitar. I was inspired by the band “Nervy” and Zhenya Milkovsky. The first song I learned was “Rayony-Kvartaly” by the band “Zveri”. Playing guitar helps me relax and unwind.

In September 2024, I started taking hip-hop classes. I really liked the group, and one of the highlights was performing at a competition. Later I moved to Germany and had to stop dancing.

In March 2025, I started attending a German school and met close friends. I especially liked cooking and technology classes, and English and German came most easily to me.

I easily make friends, so I have many friends. I especially value openness, kindness, and support in people. Friendship is very important to me, and home is the people around me with whom I feel comfortable.
